Truco Multiplayer

How to Play Truco

Truco is a popular card game played between 2 to 6 players in teams. Here are the basic rules:

Game Setup

  • Players are divided into two teams
  • Each player receives 3 cards per round
  • The goal is to win 2 out of 3 rounds

Card Values

Cards have special rankings in Truco. Check the Card Values section for the complete hierarchy.

Gameplay

  • Players take turns playing one card at a time
  • The highest card wins each round
  • Win 2 rounds to win the game
  • First team to 12 points wins the match

Special Moves

Players can call "Truco" to raise the stakes of the current hand. The opposing team can accept, decline, or raise the stakes further.

Card Values

Cards are ranked from highest to lowest as follows:

  1. 4 of Clubs (Zap)
  2. 7 of Hearts (Copas)
  3. Ace of Swords (Espadilha)
  4. 7 of Diamonds (Pica Fumo)
  5. All 3s
  6. All 2s
  7. All Aces (except Swords)
  8. All Kings (K)
  9. All Jacks (J)
  10. All Queens (Q)
  11. All 7s (except Hearts and Diamonds)
  12. All 6s
  13. All 5s
  14. All 4s (except Clubs)
